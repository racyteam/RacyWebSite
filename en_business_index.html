Business 1

CREATOR SOCIAL MEDIA

In Creator social media, creators, influencers, planners, producers, etc. create an account on the RACY platform to create their own social media. This allows them to post their own content in the form of live streaming, recorded streaming, photos, and blogs, and communicate with their fans through chat windows and comments. In Creator social media, creators can communicate with fans by creating content in a total of four forms as described above, and can sell their own content, corporate promotion, and product sales.

Business 2

INFLUENCER MARKETING

Open market sellers register creators of the RACY platform as influencers to market and sell their products.
Sellers can view the creator brand index provided by the RACY platform and sign a contract to register creators that
match their products as influencers. The contract is automatically concluded by the smart contract, and the link value of the product information provided by the seller is linked with the influencer's ID. Creators can start promoting their products after signing a contract, and can promote them through various marketing methods such as the RACY platform, YouTube, Instagram, and blogs.

Business 3

LINKED GIRL
(MODEL CONNECTION)

Linked Girl is a model linkage system in which an advertiser selects a creator (model) suitable for their product, content, and brand on the RACY platform and concludes a contract. There are various types of model contracts, such as a fashion-related fitting model, a streaming-type product and brand promotion model, and a concept model for a print advertisement, in which the creator takes a picture and sends it back to the advertiser.

Advertisers can select the model they like by checking the quality and content of the creator's content in advance, and they can create advertisements at very low cost without the agent cost for general model shooting and manpower for model shooting. Creators can easily shoot using the same camera as their previously uploaded content, and they can provide advertisers with content of the concept they want without moving or extra costs and earn profits.

Business 4

UNIQUE CONCEPT
OPEN MARKET

In the Unique Concept Open Market, creators can register and sell goods and digital goods they have made themselves, and sell products sold through companies connected with influencers and live commerce. Products include underwear, general products related to creators and influencers, fashion, and cosmetics. It consists of digital goods (photos, videos, pictorials, etc.) and can be registered based on the category according to the open market policy. Basically, it is the same as the general open market, and as a direct transaction system by smart contract between the seller and the buyer, the RACY platform only provides a trading place and is not responsible for mutual transactions. Therefore, the buyer must make a purchase after clearly recognizing the seller's information and reviews as in the general open market.
