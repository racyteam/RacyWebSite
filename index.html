SOCIAL MEDIA CONTENT PLATFORM

A social media content platform where
creators & production companies freely create
their own videos, photos, goods, etc.
 Blockchain based platform & RACY Token

PLATFORM

Racy Platform

The RACY platform is a social media content streaming platform where creators communicate with fans by uploading images and videos through various business models such as fashion, games, travel, live streaming, and commerce. Through this, creators, sellers, advertisers, etc. can create accounts and sell their own content, influencer contracts, model connection, product sales, etc., and users can purchase desired services and products.

Platform Structure

The platform has a structure that automatically pays and settles by connecting four business models through each smart contract.All of this is done through smart contracts and the platform token RACY is used. In addition, each business model can manage its own account, content, and revenue by each management system. In the platform, creators' contents can be sold as NFTs through easy NFT creation by linking with the NFT Market of a separate business model.

BUSINESS

Business1. Creator Social Media

In Creator social media, creators, influencers, planners, producers, etc. create an account on the RACY platform to create their own social media. This allows them to post their own content in the form of live streaming, recorded streaming, photos, and blogs, and communicate with their fans through chat windows and comments. In Creator social media, creators can communicate with fans by creating content in a total of four forms as described above, and can sell their own content, corporate promotion, and product sales.

TOKEN ECOSYSTEM

Sponsoring System

The RACY platform provides a system where users support creators, strengthening the bond between creators and fans, and providing a system that benefits each other.

Liquidity Mining system

As a part of marketing, the liquidity mining system pays RACY to the exchange for a certain period through a contract with the exchange where RACY is listed, and airdrops RACY as a reward to users with high trading volume.When a user applies for Liquidity Mining, they are entitled to receive airdrops, and at this time, they and their creators receive RACY by distributing them according to a certain ratio.
